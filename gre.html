Acropolis of Athens - Greece


• The Acropolis of Athens is an ancient citadel located on a rocky outcrop above the city of Athens, Greece, and contains the remains of several ancient buildings of great architectural and historical significance, the most famous being the Parthenon.


• Built during the Golden Age of Athens, it symbolises the birthplace of democracy. Extensive restorations are ongoing to preserve this UNESCO World Heritage Site.


• Was the center of Athen's religious life for centuries, with it becoming famous in the fifth century BCE.
