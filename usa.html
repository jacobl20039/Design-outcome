Mount Rushmore - USA


• Mount Rushmore National Memorial is a massive sculpture carved into Mount Rushmore in the Black Hills region of South Dakota.

• Completed in 1941 under the direction of Gutzon Borglum and his son Lincoln, the sculpture's roughly 60-ft.-high granite faces depict U.S. presidents George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Roosevelt and Abraham Lincoln.


• Represents the nation’s foundation, expansion, preservation, and development.


• These landmarks represent monumental human achievements, each reflecting unique architectural prowess, cultural heritage, and historical significance.


• Mount Rushmore is an iconic American monument that draws millions of visitors annually, symbolising the country’s historical legacy.
